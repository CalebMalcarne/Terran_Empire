Planets of the Epsilon Eridani System

Avalon

Description: A super-Earth with a thick atmosphere and extensive algae-covered seas.

Colonized by: Elves and Humans coexist in harmony, utilizing algae as a key resource.

Unique Feature: Algae blooms are harvested for both food and biofuel.

Nimbrethil

Description: A tidally locked exoplanet with a perpetually dark side covered in ice and a sun-facing side dominated by volcanic activity.

Colonized by: Dark Elves in the twilight zone where both extremes meet.

Unique Feature: The twilight zone is home to exotic thermal plants and creatures adapted to drastic temperature changes.
