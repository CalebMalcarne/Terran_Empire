Space Station: Orion’s Anvil

Orion’s Anvil

Description: A massive space station orbiting a magnetar, known for its dangerous and highly lucrative rare-element mines.

Function: Primary hub for mining operations, scientific research on extreme gravitational and magnetic effects, and black market trades.

Unique Feature: Equipped with advanced magnetic shielding, this station is one of the few places where artifacts from the early universe are studied.
