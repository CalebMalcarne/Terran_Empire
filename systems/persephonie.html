Planets of the Persophonie System

Persophonie

Description: A desert planet undergoing terraformation to create a more habitable environment.

Colonized by: Humans, with minor orc settlements.

Unique Feature: Giant, mobile terraforming units that slowly convert the desert into habitable land.

Iridia

Description: A rocky, mineral-rich planet with high gravity.

Colonized by: Dwarves and Orcs, focused on mining.

Unique Feature: Deep canyons with bioluminescent algae that glow in the dark.
