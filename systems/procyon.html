Planets of the Procyon System

Procyon Prime

Description: A gas giant orbiting close to its star, with a metal-rich core and extensive magnetosphere.

Colonized by: Floating research platforms studying the magnetic field and high-energy physics.

Unique Feature: Aurora-like lights are visible in the sky at all times due to intense magnetic activity.

Frostbite

Description: A frozen world with a nitrogen-heavy atmosphere and subzero temperatures.

Colonized by: Orcs, who have developed a unique culture around ice fishing and mammoth herding.

Unique Feature: The atmosphere occasionally freezes, falling as "nitrogen snow," which is both a hazard and a spectacle.

Silvershade

Description: A tidally locked planet, one face perpetually shrouded in darkness and covered in ice, the other scorched by the sun.

Colonized by: Drow and other nocturnal races inhabit the twilight zone, utilizing the stark temperature differences.

Unique Feature: The twilight zone harbors unique flora that blooms only during the brief twilight periods.
