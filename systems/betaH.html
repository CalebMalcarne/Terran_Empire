Planets of the Beta Hydri System

Caelum

Description: A super-Earth with an extremely thick atmosphere and constant, intense storms.

Colonized by: Automated mining drones operated remotely by Gnomes.

Unique Feature: Famous for its sky-mines, harvesting rare gases and minerals from the atmosphere.

Stormwatch

Description: A gas giant with violent atmospheric conditions and hundreds of moons.

Colonized by: Research stations floating high above the cloud layers.

Unique Feature: The planet has a permanent storm larger than Earth, known as "The Eternal Maelstrom".

Gallant

Description: A barren world, bombarded by intense radiation from a nearby pulsar.

Colonized by: No permanent settlements, only temporary scientific outposts.

Unique Feature: Surface glows faintly due to radioactive decay stimulated by pulsar radiation.
