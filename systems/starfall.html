Astronomical Feature: Starfall Archipelago

Starfall Archipelago

Description: A series of small planetoids that orbit close to each other, forming a gravitational ballet that causes continuous meteor showers between them.

Function: A popular destination for thrill-seekers and romantics, also studied by astrologers for celestial predictions.

Unique Feature: The meteors are enchanted, causing magical effects wherever they land, such as spontaneous growth of plants, temporal loops, or bursts of wild magic.
