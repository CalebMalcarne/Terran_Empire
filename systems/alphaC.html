Planets of the Alpha Centauri System

Helios Duo

Description: A rare binary planet system orbiting a dual star. Both planets are tidally locked, with one side perpetually facing the stars.

Colonized by: Elves, who have constructed massive solar arrays to power their cities.

Unique Feature: The Twilight Ring, a habitable zone between the two extremes, rich in unique flora and fauna.

Sentinel Prime

Description: A large gas giant with a prominent ring system and many moons.

Colonized by: Gnomes on floating platforms in the upper atmosphere.

Unique Feature: Moons are used as natural research stations and defense outposts.
