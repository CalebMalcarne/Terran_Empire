Planets of the Sigma Draconis System

Drakon

Description: A planet with dense jungles and active volcanoes, rich in exotic wildlife.

Colonized by: Dragonborn, who worship the natural volcanic activity as divine.

Unique Feature: Native species of dragons that are smaller but more intelligent than their mythical counterparts.

Myrth

Description: An ocean world with scattered archipelagos.

Colonized by: Merfolk and Tritons, who live in underwater and surface floating cities.

Unique Feature: The entire planet is a gigantic coral reef, evolving into breathtaking colors and shapes.
