Planets of the Sol System

Terra Nova

Description: Earth-like planet with vast oceans and sprawling continents. Diverse biomes with a temperate climate.

Colonized by: Humans. It's a major hub of commerce and governance.

Unique Feature: Home to massive bioengineered "Sky Forests" floating in the atmosphere.

Aurelia

Description: A dense, Venus-like planet with extreme temperatures and acidic clouds.

Colonized by: High-tech Dwarf enclaves that live in aerostat habitats.

Unique Feature: Famous for its sky cities and lightning farms that harvest electrical energy.

Frostgard

Description: A cold, Mars-like planet with icy deserts and occasional liquid water rivers.

Colonized by: Dwarves specializing in cryo-mining.

Unique Feature: Contains a network of subterranean lava tubes, some of which are turned into warm oases.
